Project Overview

The posture correction belt is an innovative wearable device designed to help users maintain proper spinal alignment, reducing the risk of chronic back pain and posture-related health issues. The core of the system is built around an Arduino Uno microcontroller integrated with an MPU6050 accelerometer and gyroscope sensor. This sensor continuously monitors the orientation and movement of the upper body, specifically targeting spinal tilt or slouching.

When a deviation from the calibrated upright posture is detected and sustained for more than four seconds, the system activates a coin-type vibration motor. This motor delivers a subtle, non-intrusive vibration pulse to alert the user, encouraging them to correct their posture. The vibration is controlled in an intermittent 1-second on/off cycle to avoid discomfort while remaining effective.

Compact, lightweight, and easily integrated into an elastic chest-worn belt, the device ensures comfort and discretion for everyday use. The product is especially suitable for students, office workers, and individuals with sedentary lifestyles, offering a proactive solution to long-term spinal health.
