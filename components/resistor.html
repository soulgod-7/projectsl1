Resistor

🔧 Basic Information

  • Component: Resistor
  • Unit: Ohm (Ω)
  • Main Function: Limits current flow and divides voltage in circuits

⚡ Electrical Characteristics

  • Resistance: Varies (e.g., 1Ω, 10kΩ, 1MΩ)
  • Power Rating: Commonly 1/4W, 1/2W, 1W, etc.
  • Tolerance: ±1% (precision resistors) or ±5% (general-purpose)

📏 Physical Specs

  • Package Type: Axial leaded (through-hole) or SMD (surface-mount)
  • Color Code: Bands indicate resistance value and tolerance

⚙️ Features

  • Simple and reliable
  • Available in a wide range of resistance values
  • Used in almost all electronic circuits

🧱 Common Applications

  • Current limiting for LEDs
  • Voltage division
  • Pull-up or pull-down resistors in digital circuits
  • Biasing transistors
